# GDPR Compliant Privacy Policy

At **NoCaptchaAi.com**, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This **Privacy & GDPR Compliance Policy** outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and other applicable privacy laws. We are dedicated to ensuring that your data is handled responsibly, securely, and transparently.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect both **personal** and **non-personal** information to provide and improve our services. The types of data we collect are outlined below:

### Personal Information
- **Account Registration**: When you register for an account or use our services, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, and other contact information.
- **Communication**: If you contact us via email or other methods, we collect the information you provide, including your name, email address, and message content.
- **Payment Information**: For payments made through our service, we collect payment-related information. However, **NoCaptchaAi.com** does not store your payment details directly; these are processed securely by third-party payment providers.

### Non-Personal Information
- **Usage Data**: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website and services, such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and usage patterns.
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. For more details on how cookies are used, please refer to our [Cookie Policy](/legal/privacy#8-cookies-and-tracking-technologies).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and non-personal information for the following purposes:

- **To Provide Our Services**: Your data is used to operate and deliver our CAPTCHA-solving services, including any related functionality or support.
- **Communication**: We use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, updates, new features, or respond to your inquiries.
- **Service Improvement**: We analyze usage data to improve our services and optimize your user experience.
- **Legal Compliance**: We may process your data to fulfill our legal obligations, including responding to DMCA notices, abuse reports, and other necessary actions.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data (GDPR)

In accordance with GDPR, we process your personal data only when we have a valid legal basis. The following are the legal grounds on which we process your data:

1. **Consent**: You have given us explicit consent to process your data for specific purposes (e.g., signing up for an account, subscribing to newsletters).
2. **Contractual Necessity**: Processing is necessary to perform a contract with you or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract (e.g., providing services).
3. **Legitimate Interests**: We may process data for legitimate interests, provided these interests do not override your rights and freedoms (e.g., improving our services).
4. **Legal Obligation**: We may process data when necessary to comply with a legal obligation, such as responding to government or regulatory requests.

## 4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

- **Encryption**: We use encryption protocols to protect sensitive data during transmission.
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel have access to your data, and we implement strict access control mechanisms.
- **Secure Storage**: Your data is stored securely on servers with appropriate security features to protect against data breaches.

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. However, we may share your data under the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We may share your data with third-party service providers who assist in the operation of our website and services (e.g., hosting, payment processing). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
- **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms of service, or respond to legal requests from public authorities.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user located within the European Union or the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

1. **Right to Access**: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
2. **Right to Rectification**: You can request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold.
3. **Right to Erasure**: You can request the deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
4. **Right to Restrict Processing**: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain situations.
5. **Right to Data Portability**: You can request that we provide your personal data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service provider.
6. **Right to Object**: You can object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes or based on legitimate interests.
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: If you have provided consent for processing, you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [privacy@nocaptchaai.com](mailto:privacy@nocaptchaai.com).

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy & GDPR Compliance Policy. Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention policy.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website and analyze usage patterns. Cookies help us remember your preferences and enhance the functionality of our services.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some features on our website. For more detailed information on cookies, refer to our [Cookie Policy](#).

## 9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside of the European Economic Area (EEA), your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of the EEA. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy & GDPR Compliance Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated to you via email or a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your personal data.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy & GDPR Compliance Policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us at:

- **Email**: [privacy@nocaptchaai.com](mailto:privacy@nocaptchaai.com)

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.
