## How to File a DMCA Complaint

To file a valid DMCA notice, please provide the following information:

### 1. **Your Contact Information**
   - Full Legal Name
   - Physical Address
   - Telephone Number
   - Email Address

### 2. **Identification of the Copyrighted Work**
   - Clear identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed upon. Please provide specific details such as URLs or other identifying information.

### 3. **Identification of the Infringing Material**
   - Clear identification of the material you believe is infringing upon your copyrighted work. Include URLs or any other identifying details of the automated CAPTCHA recognition service(s) in question.

### 4. **Good Faith Belief Statement**
   - A statement under penalty of perjury that the use of the material in question is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

### 5. **Signature**
   - A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf.

Once we receive your complaint, we will review the information provided and take appropriate action as required by law.

## Counter-Notification Process

If you believe that material was removed or disabled due to a mistake or misidentification, you may file a counter-notification by providing the following information:

### 1. **Your Contact Information**
   - Full Name, Address, Telephone Number, Email Address

### 2. **Identification of the Removed Material**
   - Clear identification of the material that was removed or disabled, along with the location where it appeared prior to removal.

### 3. **Statement Under Penalty of Perjury**
   - A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed as a result of a mistake or misidentification.

### 4. **Jurisdiction Consent**
   - Consent to the jurisdiction of the U.S. District Court for the district where you reside or where **NoCaptchaAi.com** is located. You must agree to accept service of process from the party who submitted the DMCA complaint.

### 5. **Signature**
   - A physical or electronic signature of the person filing the counter-notification.

Please send your counter-notification to:

[dmca@nocaptchaai.com](mailto:dmca@nocaptchaai.com)
