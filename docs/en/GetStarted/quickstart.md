## Step 2: Install [Chrome Extension](https://github.com/noCaptchaAi/noCaptcha_extension)

---

Solve hCaptcha and reCaptcha with noCaptchaAi Chrome extension. Completely Open source and It's free and easy to use. For Chromium browsers (Chrome, Chromium, Opera, Brave, Edge, Vivaldi, etc.)

---

### **Download ZIP**

- Download zip and extract > visit `chrome://extensions` > load unpacked > select folder > done

![chrome://extension](/extapi.png)

### **Download CRX**

- Download crx and drag over `chrome://extensions` page > done
